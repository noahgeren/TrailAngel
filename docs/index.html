Trail Angel

What is it?

Hiking is a fun hobbie, but it can also be a dangerous one. Just in case something bad happens it is best to let someone know where you are going to be so that they can send help. However it isn't always easy to remember to let your friends and family know where you are. This can be especially frustrating if you remember once you've reached the trail, but you no longer have cell service. This is where Trail Angel comes in:

Trail Angel is an android app that allows you to schedule hikes in advanced so that your friends and family can always know that you're safe.

How does it work?

  1. Pick from a list of known trails that we have precompiled information for.
  2. Schedule the time and date that you are going the be hiking the trail.
  3. Enter the name and phone numbers of the people you want contacted while hiking.
  4. Now, before and after the hike, those people will get texts telling them where you are.

How much does it cost?

Trail Angel is completely FREE.
